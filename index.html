ABOUT US


The DOST-NEUST Technology Business Incubator (TBI) is part of the DOST-PCIEERD HEIRIT Program, established on March 27, 2023 through a collaboration between NEUST and the Department of Science and Technology. It promotes technopreneurship and innovation in Region III, supporting startups, students, faculty, researchers, and MSMEs through resources for innovation and business growth.

The project was proposed by Dr. Kenneth L. Armas, and by 2025, it transitioned under the Innovation Management Office, now led by Dr. Edgelly G. Vitug. Today, the TBI continues to foster idea incubation, product development, and technology commercialization, driving sustainable innovation and national progress.
